Hit Counter Tumblr

This video tutorial will show you how to add a hit counter to your Tumblr blog. This is the most easy way: editing the theme description.

The process is very easy. Choose your counter, copy the generated code, go to your tumblr and edtit the theme. Paste the hit counter code in the description field.

Add the hit counter elsewhere

If you want your hit counter appears in any other location rather than the description, you can edit your theme html code.

The nex video show you how you can do it. In the video we also add the hit counter bellow the description, but you can add it wherever you want.

Footer

If you want your hit counter appears at the footer, then this is your video tutorial.

You have to edit the theme html code. Look for 'id="footer', or footer, ...
